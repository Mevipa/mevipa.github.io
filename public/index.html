Dr. Melanie Viola Partsch

After completing my PhD as a research associate at the GESIS Leibniz Institute for the Social Sciences in Mannheim and the University of Education Weingarten, I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Methodology and Statistics at Utrecht University. My research focuses on psychometric methods, particularly the development of machine-learning-based approaches for detecting and classifying misspecifications in structural equation models. I am also interested in applied psychometrics, especially the measurement and hierarchical modeling of character, values, and personality constructs. I am a member of the Measurement & Machine Learning Lab group.
